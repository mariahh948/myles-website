About Me

What's up! I've always loved the creative space and have tried to immerse myself in various forms of art and media. I grew up in the church, where I found my love for videography. I started my journey by recording, editing, and creating graphics of the church service then I eventually branched off into other things. Nowadays, I work with music artists, podcasters, business owners, and create mock commercials. My goal is to help everyone succeed and reach their audience through the various means of media.
